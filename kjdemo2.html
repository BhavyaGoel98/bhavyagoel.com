    Our Story

    Where Tradition
    Meets Mastery

    Founded in 2003, Keshavi Jewellers was born from a single conviction — that every piece of jewellery should tell a story as precious as the metals from which it is made. Rooted in India's rich goldsmithing heritage, our artisans blend centuries-old techniques with contemporary sensibilities.

    From our workshop to your wrist, every creation passes through hands that have spent decades perfecting the art of fine jewellery. We believe in creating not just ornaments, but heirlooms — pieces destined to be passed down through generations.
